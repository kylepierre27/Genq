What is GenQ?

Built for builders.
Not browsers.

GenQ is a free, verified platform for students who have decided that working for themselves is the only acceptable outcome — whether that means building a startup, trading markets, growing a brand, or creating any form of independent income.

Every feature was built around the specific conditions research shows actually produce people who successfully work for themselves. This is not a social network — it is a place to build.

The Problem

Stanford. MIT. Harvard.
The room matters.

Elite universities produce a disproportionate share of the world's most successful independent builders — not because their students are smarter, but because of who those students are surrounded by every day. The proximity. The peer pressure to build. The networked knowledge.

"The talent is everywhere. The room is not."

The Platform

Five features.
One mission.

📋
Builder Feed
Post your project title with a brief description and vision. Browse projects from builders inside and outside your network. Hit the + to apply to any project you're aligned with — the owner reviews your fit before you're in.
🤝
Builder Match
Evaluate profiles and match with people you're genuinely aligned with. No profile pictures — just resumes and ideas — so the best person for the work rises to the top, not the most polished photo.
🏗️
Build Projects
Every project gets its own meeting space for virtual calls, a dedicated project chat, and a blank collaborative workspace where the real work happens. Create or join a project and start building from day one.
💬
Builder Inbox
Message other builders directly within your network. Manage connection requests from people who found your profile and want in. Your inbox is where relationships that turn into projects begin.
👤
Builder Profile
Your profile is your signal to the platform. Edit it to reflect your skills, goals, and what you're building toward — the more complete it is, the better your matches and the stronger your first impression to project owners.
